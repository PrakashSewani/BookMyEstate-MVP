# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Home buyers and property seekers in India (tier-1 cities) who want to discover properties through an immersive, browsing-first experience without filling out long search forms. Secondary: admins managing the property catalog and analyzing user preferences.

## Product Purpose
Automatically learn what a user wants (location, type, budget) from their browsing behavior, without asking them to fill filters. Present properties in an Instagram Reels-style vertical feed that makes discovery feel effortless and addictive.

## Brand Personality
Premium, effortless, confident. Think: the sleek confidence of a luxury real estate brand meets the addictive scroll of TikTok. Not playful, not corporate — quietly premium.

## Anti-references
- Zillow/99acres search grids (clinical, filter-heavy, overwhelming)
- Generic SaaS dashboards with card grids and metric hero sections
- Cream/beige AI-default color palettes
- Overly playful or bubbly property apps

## Design Principles
1. **Show, don't ask** — Infer preferences from behavior, never force form-filling
2. **Immersive discovery** — Every property gets the full-screen, full-attention treatment
3. **Effortless flow** — Zero friction between seeing and acting; one thumb, one swipe
4. **Premium restraint** — Dark, confident palette; let the property images do the talking
5. **Behavior is truth** — What users do matters more than what they say

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Keyboard navigation for desktop users (arrow keys, shortcuts)
- Reduced motion alternatives for all animations
- Semantic HTML where possible within the immersive UI
- Sufficient contrast ratios for text overlays on images
